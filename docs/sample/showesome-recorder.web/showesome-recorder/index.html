Powerful Features

Everything you need for professional screen recording

1080p @ 60fps

Crystal-clear recordings with professional quality. Support for VP9/VP8 codec.

Multiple Recording Modes

Screen only, camera only, or both combined. Perfect flexibility for any recording scenario.

Focus Mode

Revolutionary feature that highlights and zooms into specific page elements during recording. Click any element to focus on it with beautiful spotlight effects and smooth zoom animations. Perfect for tutorials, demos, and presentations where you need to guide viewer attention to specific parts of your screen. The spotlight effect creates a professional cinematic look while keeping the rest of the screen visible with a subtle blur.

Interactive Tutorial

Virtual Backgrounds

Transform your recordings with blur effects or choose from 8+ professional backgrounds including images and videos. Real-time preview in the camera bubble lets you see exactly how you'll look before recording. Perfect for maintaining privacy, creating professional presentations, or adding creative flair to your videos. Change backgrounds during preview for camera-only recordings and apply the perfect look to match your content style.

Interactive Tutorial

High-Quality Audio

48kHz stereo recording with noise suppression for crystal-clear sound quality.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Boost your productivity with powerful keyboard shortcuts. Start or stop recording instantly with Alt+Shift+S, pause and resume with Alt+Shift+P, or cancel anytime with Alt+Shift+C. Work faster without ever touching your mouse.

MP4 Conversion

Built-in FFmpeg integration for seamless WebM to MP4 conversion. Universal compatibility guaranteed.

Smart Recordings Library

Organize all your recordings with search, filter by mode, and multiple view options.

Built-in Preview Player

Preview recordings with full playback controls. For camera-only recordings, change virtual backgrounds in real-time.
